Home Security™ Orb Documentation

What does it do?

Description

The HS Orb scans for people within a range you specify then reports these in text above the device. It can also, if you want Push, Teleport Home and Eject from land people using a target list or alternatively not on the authorised list.

The nature of the Linden Scripting Language limits the HS:
  • to detecting and identifying only the closest 100 avatars.
  • to only allowing Teleport, Land Eject and unsitting (before Pushing) if its owner (avatar or group) is also the land owner (avatar or group).
  • to scanning only within its Region.
